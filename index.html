Safety Measures for People Scared of Dogs

If you are scared of dogs, there are some safety measures you can take to feel more comfortable around them.

  • Learn about dog body language.
  • Avoid direct eye contact with dogs.
  • Don't run away from dogs.
  • Speak in a calm and reassuring voice.
  • If a dog approaches you, stand still with your hands at your sides.
  • If a dog starts to bark or growl, leave the area immediately.

It is also important to remember that not all dogs are aggressive. Many dogs are friendly and love to be petted. If you are feeling nervous around a dog, it is always best to ask the owner if it is okay to pet the dog.

With time and patience, you can learn to overcome your fear of dogs.
